Privacy Policy

Ultimate Piano · Last updated: 30 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ultimate Piano ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Ultimate Piano runs entirely on your device and does not have any backend server of its own. We do not operate a server that collects, stores, or processes your personal data. The only third-party service that may process limited data is our advertising provider, Google AdMob, as described below. By installing or using the App, you agree to the practices in this policy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or other directly identifying personal information to use the App.

  • The App has no backend server of its own — we do not collect or store your data on any server we control.
  • Your music recordings and saved performances stay on your device.
  • We do not upload your recordings anywhere.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The following data is created and stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us:

  • Recorded performances — note/event recordings ("Record Keys") and rendered audio files ("Rec As Sound", saved as AAC/M4A/WAV).
  • App settings & preferences — selected instrument, volume, and other in-app options.

You can delete this data at any time by removing recordings within the App or by uninstalling the App.

3. Permissions We Use

  • Storage / Files — to save and load your recordings on your device.
  • Audio output — to play piano and other instrument sounds.
  • Network access — used by third-party advertising and analytics services (see below) and to serve in-app content.

Note: "Rec As Sound" records the App's own synthesized audio output, not your microphone.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob, a service provided by Google LLC. AdMob is the only third party that may collect data through the App. To serve and measure ads, AdMob may collect and process information such as your device's advertising ID, IP address, device type and settings, approximate (non-precise) location, and ad-interaction data. AdMob may use this information to show personalized or non-personalized ads, depending on your settings and consent.

We do not control the data collected by Google and do not receive your personal information from these ads. Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's own privacy policy:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services

You can control ad personalization through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → reset your advertising ID or opt out of Ads Personalization.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

6. Data Security

Because your recordings and settings are stored locally on your device, their security depends on your device's own protections (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We encourage you to keep your device secure and up to date.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region (for example, under GDPR or CCPA), you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it, and to opt out of personalized advertising. Since most data stays on your device, you can exercise many of these rights directly by managing or deleting content within the App. For anything else, contact us using the details below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
